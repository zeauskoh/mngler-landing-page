The very first step

Download and set up profile.

Once you download Mngler either on the iOS App Store or the Google Play Store, you can then use your email to create an account. If you choose to use your school email (that ends in edu.sg), your school will automatically be included in your profile.

Once you provide an email, you can immediately start swiping through other profiles to get inspired, ask questions or log activities.

going further

Max out your profile.

For the algorithm to customise the right opportunities for you, continue engaging with content on Mngler.

To boost your profile so others will want to connect with you and ask you questions, include more details about your past achievements and your interests.
